I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Automation Department at Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), under the supervision of Professor Haoyao Chen. My research interests include embodied intelligence and dexterous manipulation, particularly leveraging tactile perception to enhance robot autonomy.

Our team is focusing the research on multiple intelligent robotic systems and the Internet of Things. We believe that in the near future, everything can be networked. Please visit our lab website for more information.
